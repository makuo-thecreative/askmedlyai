What is AskMedlyAI?

AskMedlyAI is a smart digital assistant that provides fast, AI-powered responses to basic medical questions while offering access to real human support when needed. The platform is designed for anyone seeking simple, reliable health information—especially in underserved communities.

Why AskMedlyAI?

  • Medical Questions, Simplified: Ask questions anytime and receive AI-generated answers tailored to common medical topics.
  • Talk to a Real Human (Student Doctor): For more detailed guidance, users can opt to speak directly with a medical student for a small fee.
  • Accessible + Affordable: Built with equity in mind, AskMedlyAI ensures users from different regions can access vital health support, even without a doctor nearby.

Features

  • Real-time AI medical chatbot
  • Simple, intuitive interface
  • Live call option with vetted medical students
  • Affordable micro-payment model
  • Built using Thunkable for rapid deployment and testing
